## Overview

As a <span className="highlighted">Webmaster</span>, I re-designed and helped maintain the company
<span className="highlighted">WordPress website</span>, as well as contributed to various digital
marketing initiatives pertaining to the website, company social medias, and more.

My primary task was to maintain the company website, implementing new front-end features such as
individual team member profiles, client-facing surveys and RSVP forms, and a Careers page. These
features were both designed and built using WordPress page builders alongside
<span className="highlighted">HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP</span> snippets.

One of my bigger projects in this position involved creating a new vision for the company website
alongside the company's graphic designer. This was a project spanning multiple months, from
<span className="highlighted">ideation and discovery to development and launch</span>. After
selecting a lightweight, highly customizable WordPress theme, I tailored it to the company's needs
through custom styling and interactive functionality.

This process, initially an effort to offload the bulkiness of the company website's years-old
WordPress theme, saw immediate improvements in
<span className="highlighted">load times, user experience, and PageSpeed score</span>.

On top of website development duties I was also in charge of analyzing site traffic through
<span className="highlighted">Google Analytics</span>, improving
<span className="highlighted">SEO</span> rankings through content and speed optimizations, and
running <span className="highlighted">Google Ads</span> campaigns.

When I was not handling website duties, I assisted in day-to-day
<span className="highlighted">IT responsibilities</span> such as new employee onboarding,
hardware/software troubleshooting, and inventory keeping.
